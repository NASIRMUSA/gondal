## Where to Get Real Product Images

### Option 1: Take Your Own Photos
- Use your smartphone to photograph actual products in your store
- Ensure good lighting (natural daylight is best)
- Use a plain white or neutral background
- Take photos from the front at eye level

### Option 2: Download from Nigerian E-commerce Sites
Visit these sites and save product images:
- **Jumia Nigeria**: https://www.jumia.com.ng/
  - Search for: Rice, Vegetable Oil, Milo, Pasta, Detergent
- **Konga**: https://www.konga.com/
- **Supermart.ng**: https://supermart.ng/

### Option 3: Free Stock Photo Sites
- **Unsplash**: https://unsplash.com/
- **Pexels**: https://www.pexels.com/
- **Pixabay**: https://pixabay.com/

Search terms to use:
- "rice bag"
- "cooking oil bottle"
- "milo beverage"
- "pasta package"
- "detergent soap"

### Option 4: Request from Suppliers
Contact your product suppliers and request:
- High-resolution product images
- Brand logos
- Marketing materials

## How to Replace Images

1. Save your new image with the same filename (e.g., `beverages.png`)
2. Place it in the `/home/anema/Desktop/Jalo gondal/website/` folder
3. Refresh your browser - the new image will appear automatically

## Recommended Image Specifications
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Size**: 600x600 pixels minimum
- **Background**: White or transparent
- **File size**: Under 500KB for fast loading
